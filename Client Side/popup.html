Help

When you are on google search page
  1. The application require working internet connetion.
  2. There are 4 different ways of searching in word-o-save.
    • Try to find meaning of any word on google, it will automatically reflect in the word-o-save on opening.
    • You can also search by simply clicking on a word inside blue box.
    • Another way of searching is manual searching which you can do on non-google pages.
    • There is another option for search by selecting the word on webpage and then opening the word-o-save. It automatically detect the meaning and present it in front of you.
  3. word-o-save will able to take only first word from Google's search input, so avoid writing in sentences. E.g. "Define ersatz" will not work
  4. The searched word can be saved for future references, you can save as many words as you want.
  5. The list of saved words can be seen by clicking "Show All" button even when you are offline.
  6. You can delete saved words permanently either by multiple selection using checkboxes or at once by "Delete All" button.
  7. For further details and usage of the particular word, you can visit the website directly by clicking "See on Wiktionary".
While on non-Google search page
  1. All above steps will work plus you can search for a word manually by typing it into the form.
  2. There is no auto correction, so spelling mistakes should be avoided by user itself.
