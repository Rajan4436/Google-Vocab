When you are on google search page
  1. Try finding meaning of any word on google, it will automatically reflect in the G Vocab while opening it.
  2. The searched word can be saved for future references, you can save as many words as you want.
  3. The list of saved words can be seen by clicking "Show All" button.
  4. You can delete saved words permanently either by multiple selection using checkboxes or at once by "Delete All" button.
  5. For further details and usage of the particular word, you can visit the website directly by clicking "Go to the Site".
  6. You can also search by by simply clicking on a word inside blue box.
